About

What This Site Is

TaxBench.ca (TB) is an independent editorial resource for Canadian income tax information. We publish clear, practical articles and provide free income tax estimators for both individuals and corporations.

Our audience includes Canadian individuals, incorporated professionals, small business owners, and anyone looking for straightforward tax information.

Editorial Approach

We strive for accuracy and clarity. Tax information is sourced from the Canada Revenue Agency, Finance Canada, and provincial tax authorities.

Tax rules change frequently. While we make reasonable efforts to keep content current, always verify against official CRA publications or consult a qualified tax professional for advice specific to your situation.
