Quick Tax Tips

More tips →

Claim all your medical expenses

Medical expenses can be claimed for any 12-month period ending in the tax year. Track your premiums, prescriptions, dental work, and travel for medical care. The credit applies once expenses exceed 3% of net income or $2,753 (2026), whichever is less.

Don’t forget the Canada Workers Benefit

If you earned employment or self-employment income and your net income is below approximately $24,000 (single) or $30,000 (family), you may qualify for this refundable tax credit. The CWB is not automatic—you must file your return to receive it.

Keep corporate meeting minutes current

For incorporated professionals, maintaining up-to-date corporate minutes and resolutions is essential. Dividend declarations, bonuses, and shareholder loans all require proper documentation. CRA can recharacterize undocumented transactions.
