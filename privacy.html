Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Our Commitment

TaxBench.ca respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under Canadian privacy law, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Information We Collect

Newsletter Signup

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This information is used solely to send you the newsletter you requested. We use a third-party email service provider to manage subscriptions and delivery.

Website Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage data (pages visited, time on site, browser type) through privacy-focused analytics. This data does not identify individual users and is used only to improve the site.

Tax Estimator

The tax estimator runs entirely in your browser. No income data entered into the estimator is transmitted to our servers or stored by us. All calculations happen locally on your device.

Cookies

We use a single functional cookie to remember your dark/light mode preference. No tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party cookies are used.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send newsletter emails to subscribers who have opted in
  • To respond to contact form inquiries
  • To analyze aggregate site usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

CASL Compliance

Our newsletter complies with Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). We obtain express consent for commercial electronic messages, identify ourselves in all messages, and include a working unsubscribe mechanism. You may withdraw consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or contacting us.

Data Retention

Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe, after which your information is removed from our active mailing list within 30 days. Analytics data is retained in aggregate form only.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Email newsletter platform (e.g., Buttondown, ConvertKit, or MailerLite)
  • Web hosting provider
  • Privacy-focused analytics

Each provider has its own privacy policy. We select providers based on their commitment to data protection and Canadian privacy compliance.

Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for newsletter communications
  • Request deletion of your personal information

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@cti.example.com.
